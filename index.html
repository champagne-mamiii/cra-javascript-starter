Our Mission

What's When Lambo?

When Lambo is a an NFT collection of 6 individual performance car models, that will live on the Ethereum blockchain. Token holders will be able to open the doors, sit and turn on the car, and hear the exhaust while driving. Think first player pov on your favorite driving game. Each model will have its own drop, and the drops will happen at the same time. 1,111 of each car model for a total of 6,666 individual NFTs. Each NFT will be one-of-one. The features will be various interior and exterior accessories and styles.

Giveaway

After all of the 6,666 NFTs are minted, a randomized token holder will be (publicly) chosen to receive a real life Lambo. The choosing of the winner will be executed publicly, as to maintain When Lambo's value of transparency with the community. We do not yet know what model or the exact time of the giveaway; so please join our discord for updates.

Community

When Lambo has been designed and developed by two independent developers. All models and attributes were hand created with attention to detail. Basically, we wanted to make an NFT that we want to drive ourselves. This project is and will be focused on the community, through blockchain voting and the development of a virtual world for the community to get to know each other and show off their rides at an interesction thats been taken over by the When Lambo Sideshow.

FAQ

Why When Lambo?

Why not?! Most of us lust at the idea to own a lambo, but realistically that may never happen. We wanted to create a realistic NFT that resembled the real thing, and functions like the real thing.

When will the collection drop?

We are still finishing up and planning the final details regarding dates/times of the drops, but the announcement will be coming ASAP. Follow our discord for announcements and the latest updates.

What are the rules of the giveaway?

Yes, we're giving away a Lamborghini! The rules are simple, once the entire collection of 6000 NFTs are minted and sold, we'll announce the random winner. All NFTs must be sold prior to the giveaway. Your chances of winning increase if you hold more than one NFT and there's no limit on how many NFTs you can own, but you must be in possession of your NFT at the time we announce the winner. If you choose to sell your NFT prior to the giveaway, the new holder of that NFT now becomes eligible to win. We're making this as fair and transparent as possible. We want to give back to the community as a means to say thank you for the support. We will constantly update everyone on all social platforms with the giveaway updates.

What is the Sideshow Takeover?

The Sideshow Takeover is a multiverse world in the early stages of development by When Lambo. We want to make a world where token holders can hang out with each other while showing off their rides - and we thought the most badass way to do that is to create an environment perfect for a "sideshow". The bonus? No cops to come ruin the fun.

Will the NFTs drop all in one drop? Or will I be able to choose what model I want?

The Collection will consist of 6 separate drops. Each model of car will have 1,111 one-of-one car NFTs so you can choose the model you want!

How is When Lambo ensuring security & transparency?

The creators of When Lambo are dedicated to complete transparency and security. All transactions and procedures will be open and easily accessible for the community. When Lambo will never store user data (we are figuring out a way to conduct the giveaway in this manner), and all transactions will be done solely by third party wallets, such as Metamask. this includes the giveaway, real time updates on transactions and When Lambo token auction prices, etc. We are also in the process of developing a documentation/resources page.
